Delicious Makeup Icecream

The icecream is the perfect balance of sweet and salty. It's the perfect meal to fix any relationship disputes!

Ingredients

  • 2 wafer cones
  • 4 scoops of salted caramel icecream
  • 1 tbsp caramel sauce
  • 2 tbsp crushed peanuts

Method

  1. Insert two scoops of salted caramel icecream into each wafer cone.
  2. Drizzle caramel sauce over the icecream.
  3. Garnish with crushed peanuts.
